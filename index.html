What I Offer

  • My Experience I have over 10 years’ experience working with professionals in finance, consultancy and corporate environments — with a genuine understanding of the pressures, self-doubt, and disconnection that can accompany high-performance roles.
  • My Approach My approach is relational and depth-oriented. Together, we explore how past patterns may be shaping your present — in your work, relationships, and sense of self.
  • Therapeutic Models I work integratively, drawing on Psychodynamic, Person-Centred, Gestalt, CBT and Attachment-based approaches — working collaboratively to make sense of your experience.
  • The Focus Our work together offers a space where you can feel safe enough to explore difficult feelings, understand yourself more deeply, and begin to relate differently — both to yourself and others.

While I offer practical tools, I believe lasting change comes through deeper relational work, over time.

About Me

Before Therapy

Before training as a therapist, I spent over a decade working in hedge funds and consultancy — across London, San Francisco, Australia and France. I know what it means to build a career, to perform under pressure, and to lose touch with yourself in the process.

My Philosophy

Our present is shaped by our past in ways we often can’t see. The patterns we developed early in life — in our first relationships, our families, the environments we grew up in — don’t disappear when we enter adulthood. They show up in how we work, how we love, how we handle conflict, and how we feel about ourselves when no one is watching.

Outside the Therapy Room

You’ll find me hiking, or somewhere outside — chasing that particular feeling of being small in a landscape. I think joy, awe, gratitude and genuine connection are what life is really for. That belief runs through how I work.
